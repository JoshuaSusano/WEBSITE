INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Every aspect of human life is touched by the continuously growing Information Technology (IT) landscape. There
is software and/or hardware for nearly everything that we do, be it learning in school, wer working at the office at
home, communicating with people here and abroad, accessing entertainment media, and connecting with others
through social media.

VISION

The Institute of Information Technology envisions itself to be one of
the leading institutes in responding to the challenges of the rapid in
evolvement of information technology and to keep with the demands
of local, national and eventually global competency in transforming,
migrating or resolving existing models to digital models.

MISSION

The institute of Information Technology is committed to provide equal
opportunities by developing the learners a knowledge,skills,and values,
through quality education and dynamic technology-driven systems, in a
diverse yet inclusive environment for learning, research,and community
engagement.

BEST OF CITY COLLEGE SAN FERNANDO PAMPANGA

COMPETENT AND EXPERIENCED DEAN AND
FACULTY
All of our courses are taught by qualified faculty and industry professionals who have had
years of experience in IT, and hold either a masteral or doctoral degree in an IT-related field.
